01 · About the Evening

Two generations.
One electric evening.

DoCSE's AfterParty 2026 is the night where the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Kathmandu University comes fully alive — greeting the freshers stepping into their first chapter, and honouring the seniors who built something worth celebrating. One venue. Two stories. Zero dull moments.

// 01
Welcome, Freshers
Your journey in CSE begins here. Meet your seniors, find your people, and feel the culture of DoCSE in one evening.
// 02
Honour the Seniors
Years of late nights, hard problems, and quiet breakthroughs — tonight we celebrate what the seniors have built and who they have become.
// 03
The Department
Computer Science & Engineering, Kathmandu University — a community built on curiosity, craft, and code that runs through everything.
// 04
The Evening
Lotus Palace, Shreekhandapur — the setting is right. The energy will do the rest. Come ready to have a night worth remembering.
